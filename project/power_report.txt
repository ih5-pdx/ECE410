Power Estimation Attempt
------------------------
OpenLane 2 power estimation (via the sky130 liberty power models in OpenSTA
and the switching-activity annotated gate-level netlist from Innovus/OpenROAD)
was not available in the synthesis container used for M3. The Yosys-only flow
used here does not produce liberty-annotated switching power.

The following estimates are derived from the Yosys cell count and published
sky130_fd_sc_hd characterisation data (TT corner, 1.8V, 25°C).

Static (Leakage) Power Estimate
---------------------------------
sky130_fd_sc_hd leakage: ~1 nW/cell (typical, TT 25°C)
Total cells: 1,900
Estimated leakage: ~1.9 µW

Dynamic Power Estimate (order-of-magnitude)
--------------------------------------------
Dynamic power = α · C · V² · f
  α (activity factor, estimated): 0.10 (10% of flip-flops switch per cycle
    during active MAC computation; AXI4-Lite register file switches rarely)
  C (per-cell capacitance, sky130_fd_sc_hd): ~2 fF average
  V: 1.8 V
  f: 100 MHz (10 ns clock)
  
  Dynamic power ≈ 0.10 × 1,900 × 2e-15 × 1.8² × 100e6
               ≈ 0.10 × 1,900 × 2e-15 × 3.24 × 1e8
               ≈ 0.10 × 1.23e-6
               ≈ 123 nW

Total estimated power (leakage + dynamic): ~2.0 µW + 0.123 µW ≈ ~2.1 µW

Note: This estimate is for the single-PE M2/M3 design only. The eventual M4
multi-PE systolic array will have significantly higher power (scale linearly
with PE count for dynamic; leakage scales with total cell count).
