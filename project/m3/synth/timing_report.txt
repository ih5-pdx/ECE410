Sky130 Note
-----------
The carry-ripple accumulator is the dominant timing path. On sky130_fd_sc_hd
at TT/25°C/1.8V, a 32-bit ripple-carry adder typically achieves ~8–9 ns.
The 10 ns clock period is conservative for sky130 and provides adequate margin.
If OpenLane STA shows negative slack, the fix is to pipeline the accumulator:
split into two 16-bit adders separated by a register stage (adds 1 cycle
latency but cuts combinational depth in half, targeting ~4.5 ns per stage).
